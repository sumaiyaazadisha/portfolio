Car Repairing Hub

"Car Repairing Hub" is a website that works like a junction point of several vehicle repair shops. Users can easily find any suitable verified car repair shop available in their nearby area. The app comes with tons of user facilities such as users can view the standard price chart and ratings of different workshops. Additionally, customers may compare the costs of the selected services of different shops. Subsequently, the website provides users with the flexibility to either personally deliver their vehicles to the car repair shop or opt for the convenience of the company handling the responsibility of picking up their car from their location and securely delivering it back to their home. Shop owners register using their trading license, and manage shop info.
